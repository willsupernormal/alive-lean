# Insights

Unit-scoped domain knowledge — facts, principles, and learnings
that should influence future work on this unit.

**Belongs here:** Domain knowledge that influences future decisions.
**Does NOT belong here:** Decisions (changelog), Claude patterns (auto-memory),
identity/rules (CLAUDE.md), people (status.md + 02_Life/people/),
one-off facts or status updates (changelog/status.md), production specs (tech packs).

**Gate:** Before adding, check: (a) does it influence future decisions?
(b) is it already in CLAUDE.md or another always-loaded file?
(c) is it a fact/status update rather than a learning? If b or c → don't add.
